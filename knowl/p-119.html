Last, we sometimes introduce a parameter (or new input variable), \(t\text{,}\) and write all three coordinates in terms of that new parameter. Hence the term “parametric curves”. For example, we can graph a horizontal circle at \(z=1\) using
  • \(\displaystyle x=\cos(t)\)
  • \(\displaystyle y=\sin(t)\)
  • \(\displaystyle z=1\)
